BrainRover: Understand Your Brain

BrainRover is a simulator that explores the parts of the brain that are stimulated when different actions are performed. It provides a visual mapping of the brain with a detailed explanation of what each part does. It displays neurological data, more specifically the different parts of the brain and the brain activity associated with certain actions.
